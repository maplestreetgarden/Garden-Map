How to Use the Garden Tracker

Navigating the Map

The map shows all the beds and structures in our garden. Each colored area is a zone — tap or click on any zone to open its details in the panel on the right.

Viewing Items and Logs

Each zone contains items — plants or objects. Tap an item to see its details and log history. Logs are dated notes that track growth, maintenance, and observations over time.

Adding Items and Logs

Tap a zone, then tap + New Item to add a plant or object. Once you have an item, tap + New Log to record an update. You will need to select a season for each log entry.

Filtering by Season

Use the season chips at the top of the screen to filter what you see by growing season. Select All to see everything at once.

Need Help?

If you have any questions or run into issues, please reach out to our garden admin Faye who manages the app and can assist with anything you need.
